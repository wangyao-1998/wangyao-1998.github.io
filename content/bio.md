I am a Postdoctoral Research Fellow at [Nanyang Technological University, Singapore](https://www.ntu.edu.sg/), working with Associate Professor [Fei Xunchang](https://dr.ntu.edu.sg/entities/person/Fei-Xunchang) on sustainable solid waste management, with a particular focus on greenhouse gas emissions from waste systems, waste-climate interactions, the environmental fate and transport of pollutants, and the long-term behavior of plastics and microplastics.
