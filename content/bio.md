I am a Postdoctoral Research Fellow at [Nanyang Technological University, Singapore](https://www.ntu.edu.sg/), working with Associate Professor [Fei Xunchang](https://dr.ntu.edu.sg/entities/person/Fei-Xunchang) on sustainable waste management, landfill biogeochemistry, and greenhouse-gas mitigation.

My research focuses on understanding the cradle-to-grave fate of waste and translating that knowledge into lower-carbon, lower-pollution waste management systems. I combine bottom-up inventories, interpretable machine learning, field observations, and process-based models to study municipal solid waste, landfills, dumpsites, greenhouse gases, and plastics.

I received my Ph.D. in Environmental Engineering from [Nanyang Technological University, Singapore](https://www.ntu.edu.sg/) in January 2026. Before that, I completed an M.Eng. at NTU and a B.S. in Environmental Science at [Tongji University](https://www.tongji.edu.cn/eng/).
