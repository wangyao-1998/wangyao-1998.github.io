I am a Postdoctoral Research Fellow at Nanyang Technological University, working with Associate Professor Xunchang Fei on sustainable waste management, landfill biogeochemistry, and greenhouse-gas mitigation.

My research focuses on understanding the cradle-to-grave fate of waste and translating that knowledge into lower-carbon, lower-pollution waste management systems. I combine bottom-up inventories, interpretable machine learning, field observations, and process-based models to study municipal solid waste, landfills, dumpsites, greenhouse gases, and plastics.

I received my Ph.D. in Environmental Engineering from Nanyang Technological University in January 2026. Before that, I completed an M.Eng. at NTU and a B.S. in Environmental Science at Tongji University.
